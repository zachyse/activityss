Bonding with Friends

March 2026 | Personal Experience

This day was special because nakapag-bonding kami ng friends ko outside school. Nag-meet kami sa SM North, naglakad-lakad, nagkwentuhan, at nagpicture-picture. Kahit simple lang, sobrang nakaka-relax siya from school stress.

After that, pumunta kami sa isang Japanese themed place. Na-enjoy namin yung ambiance at culture vibes. Mas naging fun yung experience kasi sama-sama kami.

Pinaka-highlight ng day yung samgyupsal bonding. Habang nagluluto at kumakain, tawa lang kami nang tawa at nag-share ng future plans. Nakaka-remind siya na importante ang friendship lalo na sa college life.
